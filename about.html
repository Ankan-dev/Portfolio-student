About Me

As a dedicated frontend developer with over 3 years of experience, I specialize in creating dynamic and user-friendly web applications. My expertise spans HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, and Angular, enabling me to build responsive and aesthetically pleasing websites. I have a proven track record of successfully implementing projects such as a full stack e-commerce application with a sleek frontend using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, and a robust backend with Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB. Additionally, I developed a MERN stack web application for students to register, buy courses, and interact with teachers, featuring real-time chat functionality with Socket.io and performance tracking with Chart.js. I am passionate about staying updated with the latest web technologies and continuously honing my skills to deliver exceptional digital experiences.
